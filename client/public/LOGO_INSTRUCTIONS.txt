HOW TO ADD YOUR LOGO (neon and white-06.png)
=============================================

1. Download your logo from Google Drive:
   https://drive.google.com/file/d/1_UGBHwAXEPI9AIuotwXFNmGf6ghhPObk/view

2. Rename the file to:  logo.png

3. Place it in this folder:  client/public/logo.png

4. Open:  client/src/components/layout/Navbar.jsx

5. Change line:  const LOGO_EXISTS = false
         to:  const LOGO_EXISTS = true

That's it! Your logo will appear in the Navbar and Footer
with an automatic neon green drop-shadow glow.

RECOMMENDED: Also place a copy at client/public/logo-white.png
(a white/light version) for use in the Footer.
